Our practice area

Agency and trademark registration

We at Musalam bin Ali Balhaf Law & legal Consultation Firm, provide services of legal protection and registry of trademarks, and registering local and international agencies through cooperation with other local and foreign offices in and outside the sultanate. We also ensure that such procedures are clear and as easy as possible for our clients.

Companies’ law and foreign investment companies

We assist and inform our clients in offering companies establishment services in and outside duty free zones. Such services include issuing the required documents and notices in addition to their articles of partnership, incorporation certificate, authorisations and acknowledgments. Therefore, we at Musalam bin Ali Balhaf Law & legal Consultation Firm , make sure such procedures are clear and as easy as possible for our clients.

Insurance

If you are unaware of the insurance value, or the insurance company denies providing a suitable offer, feel free to call us and discuss the matter with one of our attorneys, they will offer you free consultation, or representation before the civil court to claim fair compensation at the honourable court.

Arbitration and mediation through Oman Arbitration Centre

Arbitration is considered throughout the world, to be the simplest instrument to resolve disputes amicably; its importance has considerably increased during the recent years. Our goal is to help our clients in resolving local and international disputes professionally, rapidly and neutrally. Oman Arbitration Centre was founded according to royal decree No. 2018/26. It is incorporated, financially and administratively independent. Its head quarter is in Muscat. The centre’s vision is in line with the goals of Oman vision 2040, as it is one of the instruments to encourage investments in the Sultanate of Oman.
